How to Play

Tunde is an adventerous boy. He enjoys swimming and wants to get to the river at every single oppourtunity he gets but..... the road is creeping with bugs who do not want to ensure Tunde gets to the river for a swim.

You have to find the best path to get Tunde to the river and swim every time he gets to his start position. Tunde has only five lives. Any time he collides with a bug, he loses a life and for every time he successfully gets into the river he earns 10points. After each successful attempt to get to the river, the bugs decide to go a lot faster than they did before to eliminate Tunde.

Using your up, down, left and right keys on your keyboard to move Tunde forward, backwards, towards the left or towards the right respectively and space bar key to pause the game...

HELP TUNDE!!
